## Projects

### 1. Retail Sales & Profit Analysis

**Objective**

Analyze retail sales data to identify key revenue drivers, profitability patterns, and the impact of discount strategies on profit.

**Key Analysis**

- Sales distribution across regions and product categories
- Profitability analysis of products
- Impact of discount on profit
- Monthly sales trends

**Key Insights**

- Technology category generated the highest profit.
- Certain products such as tables produced negative profit.
- High discounts significantly reduced profitability.
- Sales showed seasonal patterns toward the end of the year.

**Skills Demonstrated**

- Data cleaning and preparation
- Aggregation using groupby
- Business insight generation
- Data visualization

### 2. Customer Churn Analysis

**Objective**

Identify factors that influence customer churn in a telecom company and provide insights to improve customer retention.

**Key Analysis**

- Churn distribution analysis
- Contract type vs churn
- Monthly charges vs churn
- Tenure vs churn
- Payment method vs churn
- Internet service vs churn
- Technical support vs churn

**Key Insights**

- Month-to-month contracts have the highest churn rate.
- Customers with higher monthly charges are more likely to churn.
- New customers churn more frequently than long-term customers.
- Customers using electronic check show the highest churn.
- Fiber optic internet users churn more frequently.
- Customers without technical support are more likely to leave.

**Skills Demonstrated**

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Categorical and numerical analysis
- Data interpretation
- Business recommendations
